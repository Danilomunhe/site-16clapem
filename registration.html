General guidelines for contributed sessions/talks/poster

All submissions must be sent by completing the registration form. Abstracts must be submitted in English.

Contributed Session:

Contributed session organizers must submit all session participants' names and emails. They must ask the session participants to register and send the titles and abstracts of their talks by completing the registration form, including the session's title and the session organizer's name.

Talks and poster submissions:

You may submit only one abstract as the presenting author of a contributed talk/poster. The same abstract cannot be submitted for different presentation types.
Upload your contribution using the following latex/pdf templates: Latex Template/ PDF Template

Registration:

Participants must pay the registration fee until June 10, 2023 to be included in the final program.
